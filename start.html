Mine TH3

Rigel + KAWPOW

Use your TH3 Wallet address as username. Rewards are tracked by accepted shares, then automatically paid out when the minimum payout threshold is reached.

Stratum URL

Use this endpoint in your miner.

stratum+tcp://pool.th3chain.cloud:3333

Rigel Command

Replace YOUR_TH3_ADDRESS with your wallet address.

rigel.exe -a kawpow -o stratum+tcp://pool.th3chain.cloud:3333 -u YOUR_TH3_ADDRESS -p x pause

Steps

1. Open TH3 Wallet and copy your TH3 address.
2. Download or open Rigel miner.
3. Paste your TH3 address after -u.
4. Start the miner.
5. Open Miner Panel and paste your address to track workers.
