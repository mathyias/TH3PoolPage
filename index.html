Stratum

Use this pool endpoint in your miner.

stratum+tcp://pool.th3chain.cloud:3333

Rigel Miner

Replace YOUR_TH3_ADDRESS with your TH3 Wallet address.

rigel.exe -a kawpow -o stratum+tcp://pool.th3chain.cloud:3333 -u YOUR_TH3_ADDRESS -p x pause

How To Mine

1. Open TH3 Wallet and copy your wallet address.
2. Download or open Rigel miner on your mining machine.
3. Paste your TH3 address after -u.
4. Start the miner and watch rewards in Wallet / Explorer.

Mining Rewards

Fresh mining rewards are coinbase outputs. They appear in wallet history as mining rewards, but stay immature until enough confirmations pass. Mature rewards become spendable UTXO.

Coinbase reward appears in history
Immature rewards wait for confirmations
Mature rewards can be sent from TH3 Wallet
